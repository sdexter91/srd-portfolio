About Me

Hello! I’m Sam.

I’m a web designer and developer.

I specialize in helping small businesses in the tourism industry reach their target audience by designing and building easy-to-navigate, SEO-optimized, custom websites. Using a holistic approach, I am well-versed in the entire brand and website creation processes.

I’ve worked hard to develop an eye for detail and enjoy working closely with my clients every step of the way. Handling everything from establishing user personas and defining brand personality to wireframing design concepts and building responsive websites from scratch, I’m dedicated to finding creative ways to meet my clients’ needs.

I’m excited by the wild world of web design and development and love the challenge of learning new skills and discovering different tools to deliver the best solutions for my clients.

As a traveling remote worker, I’ve spent the past several years hopping from country to country exploring new destinations. When I’m not at my computer, you can usually find me chowing down on delicious street food or kicking back at the nearest beach.
